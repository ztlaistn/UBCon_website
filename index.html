UBCon XXXI has ended. However, there will be another awesome event happening soon!



Are you missing con and looking for something else fun to do? Come to UBCosplay's Cosmic Cosplay Ball, April 29th at 6:30pm, in SU 145!


We'll be having:

-A Swap Meet! Bring your old nerd merch and trade it for something new.

-Free play video games! Wii Sports, Dance Dance Revolution, Shrek Superslam, and Nintendo Land.

-A 3DS Streetpass meetup! Bring your 3DS!

-Pizza, snacks, and soda!


Cosplay or fancy dress is encouraged but not required. Entry is $5 per person. $3 for students.

If you're not sure where SU 145 is, it's where the vendor hall was during UBCon.
