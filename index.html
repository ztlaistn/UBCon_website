Nerf War Info

UBCon's annual Nerf War continues this year. With our classic Red and Blue teams clashing in Knox/the back of the Student Union. Running from 11:00pm to 1:00am on Friday and Saturday, we'll be playing a variety of game modes, including Team Deathmatch and Capture the Flag. This event STRONGLY encourages any form of eye protection, and all blasters must fire standard Nerf darts. We do NOT permit the use of ball firing Nerf blasters, including Nerf Rival products. The max FPS is 160/ 1 joule. Modified blasters are welcome, however they must be submitted for inspection and chronograph by the head ref. Lineup starts at 10:30 PM Friday and Saturday nights, with a cap of 75 players per team.
